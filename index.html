Education In Rwanda

The world is ready for you. Be ready for the world.

Rwanda's education motto is "Seek knowledge and serve". The Ministry of Education (MINEDUC) aims to provide equal opportunities for Rwandans to receive high-quality education through well-known learning institutions and world-class facilities. The MINEDUC's general mission is to transform Rwandans into a skilled human capital for the country's socio-economic development. The IshuriApp website, ishuriapp.com, is a free service that helps students apply to multiple Rwandan colleges. The IshuriApp is a non-profit membership organization that was founded by Paulin Clement Twakeshimana, from Petit Séminaire Ndera, to simplify the college application process. Today, more than 1,000 colleges and universities accept IshuriApp , and also students believe in it's role.
